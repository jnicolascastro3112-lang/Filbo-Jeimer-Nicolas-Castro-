📸 Special Moments from the Book Fair

Book Spotlight

"Marx y las Comunas" by José Ernesto Nováez Guerrero

This book offers a deep analysis of Karl Marx's ideas and their direct relationship with the Paris Commune of 1871. It explores themes such as collective organization, revolutionary leadership, and the power of the people. Although it is a political history book, it contains powerful lessons about management, teamwork and strategic communication.

Administrative Connection

In the field of administration, this book is highly relevant. It shows how good organization and clear leadership can change the course of history. These concepts can be applied in companies to improve team management, internal communication and crisis management. Understanding these ideas helps future administrators make better decisions.

Personal Reflection

I really liked visiting the Book Fair. Sharing this experience with my friends made it even more special. This book inspired me to think about the importance of leadership and organization in any work environment. In my future career, I want to apply these principles to become a better leader and administrator.
